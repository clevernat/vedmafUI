Welcome To VEDMAF

Our goal is to provide accurate and timely weather information to help people make informed decisions about their health and safety. We also work to improve our forecasting models and methods to better predict vector-borne diseases and other weather-related hazards.

Who We Are

Forecasters' Web (ForWEB) KNUST is an initiative of the Department of Meteorology and Climate Science, KNUST. ForWEB KNUST is mandated to prepare, disseminate and evaluate daily weather forecasts over the Ashanti Region, along with High-Impact Weather (HiW) risk indicators for the entire country.
