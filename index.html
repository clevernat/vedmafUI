Welcome To VEDMAF

Our goal is to provide accurate and timely weather information to help people make informed decisions about their health and safety. We also work to improve our forecasting models and methods to better predict vector-borne diseases and other weather-related hazards.

Who We Are

At VEDMAF, we are a team of researchers, data scientists, and public health advocates dedicated to combating vector-borne diseases through advanced forecasting models and cutting-edge technology. Our mission is to empower individuals, communities, and policymakers with accurate, actionable insights into weather patterns and their impact on health and safety. By integrating meteorology, epidemiology, and artificial intelligence, we aim to predict, mitigate, and ultimately eradicate preventable diseases like malaria. Driven by the belief that knowledge saves lives, we bridge the gap between science and society, ensuring everyone has access to critical information when they need it most.
